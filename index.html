Jinhua prefecture is home to some of China's most specialized industrial clusters, each a global leader in its category.

Yiwu

World's largest small commodity wholesale market. 75,000+ booths, 1.8M+ SKUs. Bags, jewelry, toys, stationery.

Yongkang

China's hardware capital. 70% of global thermos cup production. Drinkware, kitchenware, hardware tools.

Zhuji

Wood carving, crystal crafts, home textiles. Premium craftsmanship for home decor and gift markets.
