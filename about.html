Why Jinhua

China's most concentrated manufacturing region for consumer goods

The Jinhua Advantage

Where the World's Consumer Goods Are Made

Jinhua, located in central Zhejiang Province, is a powerhouse of Chinese manufacturing. Unlike coastal cities that focus on electronics and heavy industry, Jinhua specializes in consumer goods — exactly the products that global B2B buyers need.

With 1.26 million registered business entities and trade links to 230+ countries, the Jinhua-Yiwu corridor is one of the most dynamic manufacturing regions in the world. Ningbo port, just 2 hours away, provides efficient export logistics.

Xiangda Supply Chain was founded to bridge the gap between international buyers and Jinhua's vast but fragmented factory network. We do the legwork so you don't have to.

Industrial Clusters

Four Clusters, Unlimited Sourcing Power

Yiwu

Global Commodity Hub

Home to the world's largest wholesale market with 75,000+ booths. Dominates bags, jewelry, toys, stationery, and seasonal products. Over 1.8 million SKUs available at competitive prices.

Yongkang

Hardware & Drinkware Capital

Produces 70% of the world's vacuum flasks and thermoses. Also the #1 source for kitchenware, hardware tools, and metal products. ISO-certified factories with advanced automation.

Zhuji

World's Sock Capital

Manufactures over 30% of global socks. A dense cluster of specialized hosiery factories with Oeko-Tex and BSCI certifications. Also strong in knitwear and accessories.

Dongyang

Craftsmanship & Home

Known for wood carving, bamboo products, and home textiles. Pujiang adds crystal crafts and home decor. Premium quality for branded and gift markets.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you charge upfront fees?

A: No. We earn our margin through factory commissions and service fees only after you place an order. Initial consultation, factory matching, and sample coordination are free.

Q: What is the typical MOQ for Jinhua factories?

A: It varies by category. Jewelry: 10-50 pcs. Bags and apparel: 100-500 pcs. Drinkware: 500-3000 pcs. We can often negotiate lower MOQs for first-time trial orders.

Q: How do you ensure quality?

A: Every factory in our network is pre-audited. We perform inline inspections during production and AQL 2.5 pre-shipment inspections with detailed photo reports before every shipment.

Q: What trade terms and payment methods do you support?

A: FOB, CIF, and DDP terms available. Payment via T/T, L/C, PayPal, and credit card. Multi-currency support including USD, EUR, GBP.
